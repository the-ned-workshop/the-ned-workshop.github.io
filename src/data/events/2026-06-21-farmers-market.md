We’ll be creating simple, yet adorable (and useful!) wooden stools for all your far-reaching needs. All wood material will be pre-cut by a CNC machine and put together by YOU using pocket screws.

All of ¾ inch plywood was purchased second-hand at Resource Central in Boulder. This is beautiful, redwood plywood that will get a new life in your hands.

This workshop is designed for all ages, sizes, and genders.

Come create with us at the Farmers Market on June 21st from 10am - 2pm!

Making is free, donations appreciated when possible.
