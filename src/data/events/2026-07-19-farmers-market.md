Celebrate the joy and wonder of summer with us this Sunday (7/19) at Party in the Peaks! Located at the Ned Farmers Market from 10am-4pm (extended hours!).

Create a nature crown from recycled cardboard and nature treasures we’ve found romping around outside. All supplies provided. All ages welcome.

Making is free, donations greatly appreciated!
